Slide 4

Limitations

The prototype is strong for a hackathon, but the next priority is validity rather than more complexity.

Current limitations

  • Likely leakage: Tenure_days may use termination information unavailable at prediction time.
  • Small synthetic dataset: useful for demonstration, limited for strong external claims.
  • No temporal or external validation: results are based on one hold-out split plus internal CV.
  • Fairness evidence remains fragile: subgroup samples are small.
  • Structured-only scope: no richer HR text or broader business context.

Not intended for

  • Termination or promotion decisions made automatically.
  • Claims of objective truth about employee intent.
  • Deployment without feature redesign and validation.

Most important next step

Rebuild the feature set so every variable is available at the decision date, then retrain and measure how much performance remains without leakage.

Recommended follow-up work

ValidationUse temporal splits or another HR dataset.
FairnessRe-audit on larger subgroup samples.
FrugalityAdd explicit energy tracking.
GovernanceAdd monitoring, retraining and approval rules.

Bottom line

This is a strong explainable hackathon prototype, but production readiness depends on fixing leakage, improving validation and strengthening governance.
